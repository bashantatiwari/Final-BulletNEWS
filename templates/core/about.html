Our Story

We are a team of four passionate students who came together with a shared goal: to bridge the information gap across Nepal by creating a smarter, more accessible way to consume news. Inspired by the fast-paced lives people lead today, we noticed a common problem—staying informed takes time that many don't have. That's when BulletNEWS was born.

Challenge

In Nepal, it's difficult for people to keep up with local, regional, and national news due to scattered sources and time constraints. Long articles, slow websites, and lack of accessibility features often discourage regular news consumption.

Solution

BulletNEWS is a centralized news platform that pulls stories from across Nepal, categorizes them, and uses AI to generate short summaries. With built-in text-to-speech and speech-to-text features, it allows users to read or listen to the news in a format that suits them—perfect for busy individuals who still want to stay informed. It's fast, smart, and inclusive.

Technologies Used

Frontend HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Backend Django
Database SQlite
AI/ML HuggingFace Transformers, Gemini AI
Speech Features gTTS
